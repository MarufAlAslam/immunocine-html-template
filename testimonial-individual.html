Testimonials

Chris M.

Stage 4 Prostate Cancer

In 2014, Chris was diagnosed with Stage 4 Prostate Cancer and immediately started Chemotherapy. By 2017, Chris was in need of a different treatment option. His cancer had spread to 30 different locations throughout his body. In addition, Chemotherapy had nearly killed him as well.

After reading the early research of Immunocine’s treatment, Chris traveled to Houston to meet with the research team. With two kids who were six and eight years old, Chris was willing to do whatever it took to beat his cancer.

After receiving an earlier version of Immunocine’s Dendritic Cell Treatment, Chris has seen his PSA level go from 212 to 0. His 30 tumors were destroyed, and Chris remains in remission to this day.
